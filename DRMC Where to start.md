# Where to Start: Your Guide to the DRMC Governance Documents

**Welcome to the DRMC Governance Framework!**

We've built a comprehensive system designed to protect our community, ensure transparency, and make running the club easier for everyone. This guide is your **roadmap** to understanding what each document does and how they all fit together.

---

## The Big Picture: How It All Fits Together

Imagine our governance system as a house:

1.  **The Foundation (Unalterable):** The **Charter** is the bedrock. It establishes who we are and our core purpose. It cannot be changed without a fundamental restructuring of the corporation.
2.  **The Framework (The Rules):** The **Bylaws** are the walls, roof, and load-bearing beams. They contain the detailed rules for how we operate, from voting to finances. They can be changed, but only by a rigorous member vote.
3.  **The Daily Life (Living in the House):** This is where we spend our time. It's divided into:
    - **Policies:** High-level rules that guide our decisions (e.g., how we handle money, conflicts of interest, privacy).
    - **Procedures:** Step-by-step instructions for handling specific situations (e.g., filing a grievance, reporting an incident).
    - **SOGs & SOPs:** Standing Operational Guides and Procedures for routine, day-to-day tasks (e.g., opening the clubhouse, using equipment).
    - **Templates & Agreements:** The forms and contracts we use to put our rules into action.

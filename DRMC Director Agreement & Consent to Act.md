#### 2. CONSENT AND QUALIFICATIONS

The Director confirms their consent to act as a director of the Corporation and certifies that they meet the following mandatory qualifications under the Act:

- I am at least 18 years of age.
- I have not been found under the laws of any province or country to be incapable of managing my own affairs.
- I do not have the status of an undischarged bankrupt.
- I am an individual (not a corporation or other entity).

#### 3. FIDUCIARY DUTIES & COMPLIANCE

In accordance with the Act and the Corporation's Bylaws, the Director agrees to:

- Duty of Care: Exercise the care, diligence, and skill that a reasonably prudent person would exercise in comparable circumstances.
- Duty of Loyalty: Act honestly and in good faith with a view to the best interests of the Corporation and its regional mandate.
- Compliance: Strictly adhere to the DRMC Bylaws, the Act, and the Articles of Incorporation.

#### 4. SPECIFIC OPERATIONAL COMMITMENTS

The Director expressly acknowledges and agrees to uphold the following commitments as outlined in the Bylaws:

- The 4% Rule: To prioritize operational efficiency and never propose a fee hike exceeding 4% without providing detailed justification and obtaining approval by a majority vote of Voting Units at the September AGM, as required by Bylaws Article VI.2.
- Dual Signature Mandate: To never unilaterally commit corporate resources or sign contracts without the secondary authorized signatory.
- Anti-Privatization: To protect the Marina assets from hostile takeover or privatization, maintaining them as a public trust for the Restigouche region.

#### 5. CONFLICT OF INTEREST

The Director shall immediately disclose in writing the nature and extent of any interest in a material contract or transaction, whether made or proposed, with the Corporation. The Director shall recuse themselves from any vote concerning such contracts or transactions, in accordance with the Act and the Bylaws.

#### 6. CONFIDENTIALITY & PRIVACY

The Director shall maintain the strict confidentiality of all non-public corporate information, including member personal data (as per PIPEDA), sensitive financial accounts, and legal strategies. This obligation survives the expiration of the Director's term.

#### 7. VOLUNTEER STATUS & NO COMPENSATION

The Director acknowledges that this is a volunteer position. No financial compensation, discount on mooring/camping fees, or special privileges shall be granted in exchange for service on the Board.

#### 8. CODE OF CONDUCT & DISCIPLINE

The Director agrees to lead by example, adhering to the highest standards of decorum and respect. The Director understands that missing three (3) consecutive meetings without valid excuse results in automatic resignation.

#### 9. INDEMNIFICATION

The Corporation shall indemnify the Director against legal costs and expenses resulting from their duties, provided the Director acted in good faith and remained compliant with the law and these Bylaws. The Corporation confirms the maintenance of Director & Officer (D&O) Liability Insurance.

#### 10. TRANSITION MANDATE

Upon the conclusion of their term, the Director agrees to participate in the mandatory transition period and deliver a "Transition Package" including all relevant digital credentials, physical keys, and corporate records.

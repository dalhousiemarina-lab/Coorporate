# Standard Operating Procedure (SOP): Hik-Connect (Web Portal)

## 1.0 Purpose
To provide a structured, step-by-step guide for authorized personnel on accessing, operating, and performing basic monitoring tasks within the HIKVision Hik-Connect web portal.

## 2.0 Scope
This SOP applies to all personnel authorized to access and utilize the centralized video monitoring and device management features of the Hik-Connect platform via a standard web browser.

## 3.0 Prerequisites
- A stable internet connection.
- A modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Edge).
- Valid Hik-Connect login credentials (Username/Email and Password).
- Authorized access to the linked HIKVision devices (NVRs/DVRs/Cameras).

## 4.0 Procedure

### 4.1 Logging In to the Portal

| Step | Action | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4.1.1** | **Navigate to the Hik-Connect portal.** | Open your web browser and go to the official login URL: `https://www.hik-connect.com/views/login/index.html#/portal` |
| **4.1.2** | **Enter Credentials.** | Input your registered **Username** (or Email/Mobile Number) and **Password** in the designated fields. |
| **4.1.3** | **Verify and Log In.** | Complete any required security verification (e.g., CAPTCHA) and click the **Login** button. |
| **4.1.4** | **Initial Check.** | Upon successful login, ensure all expected devices are displayed in the device list. |

### 4.2 Viewing Live Video (Live View)

| Step | Action | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4.2.1** | **Select the Live View interface.** | On the main dashboard, click the **Live View** tab or icon. |
| **4.2.2** | **Select Device.** | On the left-hand panel, locate the desired NVR/DVR or individual camera. |
| **4.2.3** | **Drag and Drop Camera.** | Click and drag the camera channel icon from the device list onto an empty viewing pane on the screen. |
| **4.2.4** | **Adjust Stream Quality (If necessary).** | Below the video feed, click the quality setting (**Fluent**, **Balanced**, or **HD**) to switch the video resolution. Use **Fluent** for lower bandwidth situations. |
| **4.2.5** | **Full-Screen Mode.** | Click the **Full Screen** icon (usually four outward arrows) on the bottom right of the video pane for detailed viewing. Press **ESC** to exit. |

### 4.3 Searching and Playing Back Recorded Footage

| Step | Action | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4.3.1** | **Select the Playback interface.** | Click the **Playback** tab or icon on the main menu. |
| **4.3.2** | **Select Camera and Date.** | On the left panel, check the box next to the camera(s) you wish to review. Then, click on the **Calendar Icon** to select the required date. |
| **4.3.3** | **Load Timeline.** | The timeline at the bottom of the screen will load. Segments of recorded footage (usually in green or yellow for motion events) will appear. |
| **4.3.4** | **Start Playback.** | Click on the timeline bar at the specific time you wish to start viewing. Playback will begin automatically. |
| **4.3.5** | **Review Controls.** | Use the controls below the timeline for navigation: **Play/Pause**, **Fast Forward** (e.g., 2x, 4x, 8x), and **Previous/Next Day**. |
| **4.3.6** | **Clip and Download (If required).** | Use the **Clipping/Scissor** icon to select a start and end time for a footage segment. Once clipped, select the **Download** icon to save the video file locally. |

### 4.4 Basic Device and Account Management

| Step | Action | Details |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **4.4.1** | **Access Device Management.** | Click on the **Device Management** tab. This allows users to view the status, connectivity, and health of linked devices. |
| **4.4.2** | **Check Device Status.** | Verify the **Status** column for each device. "Online" confirms it is connected and recording. "Offline" requires immediate attention. |
| **4.4.3** | **Access Account Settings.** | Click on your **profile icon** (top right corner) to change passwords, update email, or configure security settings. |

## 5.0 Safety and Security Notes

- **Password Security:** Do not share your login credentials. Change your password immediately if you suspect unauthorized access.
- **Log Out:** Always log out of the Hik-Connect portal when monitoring is complete, especially on shared workstations.
- **Data Integrity:** Do not delete or modify recorded footage unless strictly authorized and documented.

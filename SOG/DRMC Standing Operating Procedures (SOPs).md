## Purpose

The purpose of these Standing Operating Procedures (SOPs) is to standardize the day‑to‑day execution of duties for specific officer roles and recurring tasks within the Dalhousie Regional Marina Club (DRMC). While the Bylaws (Article XII) define the existence and general responsibilities of officers, these SOPs provide step‑by‑step instructions to ensure consistency, preserve institutional knowledge, and facilitate smooth transitions when roles change hands.

These procedures are supplementary to the Bylaws and must be followed by all officers and volunteers performing the duties described herein.

---

The following role-specific SOPs have been established and are maintained as separate documents:

| Vice-Commodore (Vice-President) | [Vice-Commodore (Vice-President) Standing Operating Procedure (SOP)](vice-commodore-sop.md) | Executive support, facilities oversight, volunteer engagement, committee liaison |

| Treasurer | [Treasurer Standing Operating Procedure (SOP)](treasurer-sop.md) | Accounts payable/receivable, bank reconciliation, financial reporting, reserve fund tracking |
| Secretary | [Secretary Standing Operating Procedure (SOP)](secretary-sop.md) | Meeting agenda preparation, minute-taking standards, correspondence log, record keeping |

Each officer is responsible for adhering to the procedures outlined in their respective SOP. In the event of any conflict between a role-specific SOP and this master document, the role-specific SOP shall prevail for matters pertaining to that role's duties.

---

## SOP Development and Maintenance

### Adding New Role-Specific SOPs
As the club evolves, additional officer or coordinator roles may be established. New role-specific SOPs shall be developed using the existing templates and follow the same formatting, structure, and review process outlined in this master document.

### Review Process
Each role-specific SOP shall be reviewed annually by the officer holding the role, in consultation with the Executive. Revisions shall be brought to the Executive for approval and noted in the meeting minutes.

### Version Control
All SOPs shall include:
- Document version number
- Date of last revision
- Status (Draft, Approved, Archived)
- Effective date (if approved)

---

## Amendments and Review

These SOPs may be amended by the Executive as needed, provided the changes are consistent with the Bylaws. Any substantive changes should be communicated to the affected officers and noted in the next Executive meeting minutes. A full review of all SOPs shall occur annually in the first quarter.

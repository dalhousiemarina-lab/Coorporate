## SECTION 10.0: SPILL RESPONSE PROCEDURES

### 10.1 Immediate Actions

**If a spill is discovered:**

**Step 1: ENSURE PERSONAL SAFETY**
- Evacuate area if necessary
- Eliminate ignition sources
- Wear appropriate PPE
- Do not walk through spilled material
- Alert others in the area

**Step 2: STOP THE SOURCE** (if safe to do so)
- Shut down pumps
- Close valves
- Stop transfer operations
- Right overturned containers
- Plug leaks if possible

**Step 3: CONTAIN THE SPILL**
- Deploy spill kits/absorbents
- Block drains
- Create dikes/berms
- Cover drains with plastic/absorbents
- Use booms for water protection

**Step 4: NOTIFY**
- Notify supervisor immediately
- Contact emergency coordinator
- Call for additional resources if needed
- Initiate emergency contacts list

**Step 5: CLEAN UP**
- Recover spilled material
- Properly dispose of contaminated materials
- Restore affected area
- Document the incident

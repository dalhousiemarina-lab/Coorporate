# DRMC Membership & Access Classifications Summary

**Document Version: 3.0 – Effective Date: February 28, 2026**

This document summarizes the membership classes and voting rights as defined in the DRMC Bylaws (Article IV).

### Class A Voting Units (One Base Vote Each)

| Subcategory       | Agreement Held       | Base Vote | Eligible for Bonus Vote? |
|-------------------|----------------------|-----------|--------------------------|
| **Berth Holder**  | Seasonal boat slip   | 1         | Yes (with 20 volunteer hours) |
| **Seasonal Camper**| Seasonal campsite    | 1         | Yes (with 20 volunteer hours) |
| **Dual Member**   | Slip + Site          | 1         | Yes (with 20 volunteer hours) |

**Bonus Vote:** Any Voting Unit may earn one additional vote (maximum two per Unit) if at least one adult member completes 20 verified volunteer hours within the preceding 12 months. Volunteer hours are tracked in the public Volunteer Registry and expire after 12 months.

### Non-Voting Access Categories

- **Associate Member:** Social member with no boat slip or campsite. No voting rights.
- **Overnight Guest / Transient:** Short-term visitor (less than 30 days). No voting rights.

*For complete details, refer to the DRMC Bylaws, Article IV.*

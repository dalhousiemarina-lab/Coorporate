## II. Dock Closure

| Task | Details | Responsible | Done ✅ |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Notice to Campers | Send email or notice 4 weeks before dock removal: shut off water + electricity | Management / Supervisor | ☐ |
| Dock Removal | Coordinate complete dock removal (crane) | Team + Contractor | ☐ |
| Anchor Removal | Hire diver or specialized contractor to remove all anchors | Supervisor | ☐ |
| Dock Repairs | Identify holes, damaged boards, bent pins | Team | ☐ |
| | Repair and paint identified areas (marine paint) | Team | ☐ |
| Electrical Towers | Open water valves before bringing towers in (prevent freezing) | Team | ☐ |
| | Touch up tower paint if necessary | Team | ☐ |
| | Store towers in Gazebo or secure location | Team | ☐ |

## III. Winterizing

### Gazebo & Office

| Task | Responsible | Done ✅ |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Completely drain water heater and pipes (compressed air or non-toxic antifreeze) | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Open all faucets and leave open | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Drain pump and potable water lines | Plumber / Team | ☐ |

### Bathroom (main building or washroom facility)

| Task | Responsible | Done ✅ |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Drain water heater, toilets, showers, sinks | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Pour pink antifreeze (RV/marine grade) into all traps, bowls, and drains | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Open all faucets (leave open) | Plumber / Team | ☐ |

### All Campsites (water + electricity)

| Task | Responsible | Done ✅ |
|------|-------------|--------|
| Verify that each camper has shut off and drained their hoses | Management / Team | ☐ |
| Shut off main water supply at main panel (main valve) | Team | ☐ |
| Shut off main electricity for docks and sites (circuit breakers) | Team | ☐ |
| Drain water pedestals and leave valves open | Team | ☐ |
| Pour antifreeze into pedestal drains if applicable | Team | ☐ |

---

## Final Checklist

*To be completed by the supervisor*

- [ ] All doors and windows are barricaded/locked
- [ ] All movable items are stored and protected
- [ ] No faucet or valve is closed with water remaining inside
- [ ] Antifreeze has been applied wherever necessary
- [ ] Docks removed and anchors retrieved
- [ ] Photos taken (before/after) for records

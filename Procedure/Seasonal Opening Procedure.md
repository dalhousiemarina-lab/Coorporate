# Seasonal Opening Procedure 2025

## Objective
Prepare and open all facilities (Office, Gazebo, Docks, Campsites) for the upcoming season, ensuring all systems are operational, safe, and ready for use.

## II. Gazebo and Office Opening

| Task | Details | Responsible | Done ✅ |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Barricades | Remove plywood panels from all openings (Office + Gazebo) | Team | ☐ |
| Windows | Open and inspect all windows; ensure screens are intact and hardware functions properly | Team | ☐ |
| Cleaning | Thoroughly clean all surfaces, floors, and fixtures | Team | ☐ |
| Tables | Remove tables from walls and position according to layout plan | Team | ☐ |
| Chairs | Unstack chairs and distribute around tables | Team | ☐ |
| Rocking Chairs | Bring rocking chairs outside to designated areas | Team | ☐ |
| Trash Cans | Retrieve trash cans from storage and electrical shed; position at designated locations | Team | ☐ |
| Mini-Barn | Unlock and inspect; verify contents and organization | Team | ☐ |
| Appliances | Plug in and test ice machine, freezers, and all kitchen appliances | Team | ☐ |

## III. Water System Start-Up

| Task | Details | Responsible | Done ✅ |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Visual Inspection | Inspect all visible pipes, fittings, and connections for damage from freezing | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Main Valve | Slowly open main water supply valve at main panel | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| System Flush | Flush all lines to remove any residual antifreeze or debris | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Leak Check | Inspect all connections, faucets, and fixtures for leaks | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Water Heater | Fill water heater; verify proper operation before turning on power | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Faucets | Close all faucets after flushing is complete | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Potable Water System | Sanitize and flush potable water system per health regulations | Plumber / Team | ☐ |

### Bathroom (main building or washroom facility)

| Task | Details | Responsible | Done ✅ |
|------|---------|-------------|--------|
| Drains | Flush all drains with water to clear antifreeze | Plumber / Team | ☐ |

| Showers | Test shower operation and drainage | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Sinks | Test faucets and drainage | Plumber / Team | ☐ |
| Ventilation | Verify exhaust fans are operational | Team | ☐ |
